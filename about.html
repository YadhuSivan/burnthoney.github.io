Burnt Honey Dessert Company was created by wife-and-wife duo Kara and Fiona Shedden—a blend of Kara’s lifelong love for ice cream and Fiona’s bold entrepreneurial drive. Since opening our doors on February 4th, 2019, we’ve proudly crafted small-batch, high-quality ice cream and delicate macarons for our community. We’re passionate about lifting up local farmers, artists, and small businesses, and we work hard to keep our carbon footprint low by choosing compostable and recyclable materials whenever possible. Most of all, Burnt Honey is a place where everyone is welcomed and celebrated—no matter their race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, abilities, or disabilities.
